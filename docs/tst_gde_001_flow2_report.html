장르/트로프 정렬
  • System/progression readability is a primary market signal: levels, skills, growth loops, and payoff cadence should be explicit.
  • Isekai/reincarnation framing is prominent; early setup should clarify transfer/rebirth rules and the protagonist's advantage.
  • Relationship-forward fantasy/romance demand is strong; emotional stakes and pairing dynamics need clear early hooks.
  • Male-protagonist adventure/power-fantasy remains visible in platform exposure.
  • Female-lead status reversal, romance conflict, and social-position tropes are visible localization levers.
  • 반복 태그: Fantasy(248), Adventure(216), Action(212), LitRPG(164), ONGOING(140), Original(140), Progression(111), WAIT_UNTIL_FREE(100)

연령등급/민감도
  • Do not treat platform exposure order as a universal national market ranking; it is platform-specific evidence.
  • Do not use collected synopsis text as story content; summarize signals and cite platform/source/date instead.
  • 플랫폼별 노출 순서는 시장 전체가 아니라 해당 플랫폼 증거로만 표현합니다.
구체 적용 체크리스트
  • Anchor the pitch in the dominant platform-visible genre mix before adding niche cultural explanations.
  • Convert synopsis observations into title hooks, opening-episode stakes, and tag/copy choices rather than copying source descriptions.
  • Preserve progression mechanics in translation: skill names, rank terms, and upgrade cadence should be consistent across episodes.
  • Make relationship premise, power imbalance, consent boundary, and emotional payoff legible in synopsis and chapter-one localization.
  • Explain reincarnation/transport premises compactly; readers tolerate familiar setups when the unique advantage is clear.
  • For English platforms, foreground premise clarity and genre tags; avoid long cultural footnote-style exposition in the opening pitch.
  • 최종 가이드에는 사용한 플랫폼, 수집일, rank evidence를 함께 남깁니다.
  • 본문 수집 없이 공개 메타데이터와 시놉시스 신호만 사용합니다.
